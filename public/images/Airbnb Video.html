Enhance your Experience with Video

One of the easiest ways to help attract more guests to book your Experience  is by adding video. By following our step by step guide, you can film your experience and send us the video clips. Our team of editors will edit the footage and upload the video to your experience page.

Upload your Video

Step by Step to your Video

Preparation

For the highest quality, we recommend using a device newer than the iPhone 7 or a Samsung from 2016 with at least 3.5 GB of free space. We also recommend having someone else film while you present your experience on camera.

Film

Important reminder to hold your phone vertically since that is the way your final video will be shown on your experience page. Your first clip should be an introduction, talking directly into the camera, where you can share what your experience is about and who you are. Next, film two or three of the most memorable parts of your experience. And lastly, film two or three clips highlighting the final product, location or learning from your experience.

Upload

Please add as many video clips as you’d like. Our team will then edit the clips into a cohesive video for your listing page. One important note: please make sure you have permission from the people in your video and that they are aware the video will be visible to the public. Please refrain from featuring copyrighted material in your videos that you do not have the rights to feature, including photos, music and artwork.

Filming your Online Experience

In addition to filming your Online Experience like you are actually hosting it, we recommend that you include  “behind the scenes” clips, that show you interacting with  your device and guests.

Keep in mind when filming your clips

Please avoid including brand logos, recognizable artwork, large public crowds, and business names. Our professional editors will pick the right background music for your experience, so please avoid any additional music while filming. Lastly, it’s also very important to not film any guests consuming alcohol.

We are here to help

We know taking a video of your Experience is not an easy task, but we are here to support you along the way. Download our easy to follow ‘Video Guide’ for additional information and  how to connect with us at airbnbexperiencescontent@airbnb.com
